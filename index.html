who we are

Women of Dignity International (WODI) is a registered not-for-profit organization based in Canada. Our mission is to empower women through advocacy, community integration, and growth opportunities. We are passionately committed to impacting women and supporting their journey to leadership positions in society.

At WODI, we firmly believe in the power of women empowering women to create lasting change. Our vision is to see women taking up leadership roles and making a positive impact in their communities. We are dedicated to providing women with the necessary tools, resources, and opportunities to achieve personal and professional growth.

Our services are designed to address the diverse needs of women and foster their empowerment. One of our key areas of focus is immigration and legal support. We assist women in gaining legal status in Canada by providing referrals to legal services and facilitating access to immigration documents. Through our efforts, we strive to ensure that women have a smooth transition and integration into Canadian society.

Additionally, we offer newcomers' settlement services to support women, seniors, youths, and families as they navigate their new lives in Canada. Our services include helping them find suitable housing, access food banks, provide free meals, assist with resume writing, and guide them through the necessary forms for settlement. By providing these essential resources, we aim to ease their transition and foster a sense of belonging within their communities.

Another critical aspect of our work is raising awareness about abuse and providing support to women in need. We organize group sessions, presentations, and campaigns against domestic violence. These initiatives aim to educate the community, promote healthy relationships, and create a supportive environment for those affected by abuse. We firmly believe in the importance of empowering women and providing a safe space for them to heal and rebuild their lives.

At WODI, we recognize the significance of education and skills development in empowering women. We have established partnerships with colleges to offer skills assessment and career guidance programs. Through these initiatives, we encourage women to improve their self-esteem, acquire new skills, and pursue educational opportunities. We also provide access to scholarships and skill acquisition programs to enhance their professional prospects and personal growth.

Women of Dignity International (WODI) is an esteemed organization based in Canada, making a significant impact globally. Committed to promoting dignity, empowerment, and equality for women, WODI transcends borders to uplift women in communities worldwide. With a focus on women's human rights and inclusive development, WODI works tirelessly to create a world where every woman can live a life of dignity and fulfillment. Through their dedicated efforts, WODI provides support, resources, and advocacy to empower women and foster positive change. With a steadfast dedication and a global perspective, WODI stands as a catalyst for transformation, advocating for the rights and well-being of women, regardless of their background or circumstances.

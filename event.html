Mercy Itohan Osayi, President and Founder of Women of Dignity International (WODI), Receives "2023 Promising Entrepreneur Award" by The African Descent Ontario, Canada

Ontario, Canada - August 4, 2023

Women of Dignity International (WODI) proudly announces that its visionary leader, Mercy Itohan Osayi, has been recognized as the recipient of the prestigious "2023 Promising Entrepreneur Award" by The African Descent Ontario, Canada. This well-deserved honor is a testament to Mercy's relentless dedication to empowering women and transforming lives through her impactful entrepreneurship journey.

As the President and Founder of WODI, Mercy Itohan Osayi has been a driving force behind the organization's commitment to advancing gender equality and promoting women's empowerment globally. Under her visionary leadership, WODI has emerged as a trailblazing non-profit organization, championing the rights and dignity of women from all walks of life.

Mercy's journey as an entrepreneur has been nothing short of inspiring. Her unwavering determination and innovative approach have resulted in the successful implementation of numerous initiatives aimed at uplifting women and providing them with the tools and resources to achieve economic independence.

Through WODI's various programs and initiatives, Mercy has played a pivotal role in empowering women entrepreneurs, especially those from marginalized communities, with mentorship, skill development, and access to funding opportunities. Her work has not only impacted the lives of countless women but has also fostered a culture of empowerment and inclusivity within the communities she serves.

In response to the news of receiving the "2023 Promising Entrepreneur Award," Mercy expressed her gratitude, stating, "I am deeply honored and humbled to receive this prestigious award from The African Descent Ontario. It is a recognition of the collective efforts of the entire WODI team and our unwavering commitment to creating a world where every woman can thrive and realize her full potential. This accolade will further fuel our dedication to breaking barriers and transforming lives."

The "2023 Promising Entrepreneur Award" recognizes Mercy Itohan Osayi's outstanding achievements as a trailblazing entrepreneur and her significant contributions towards fostering socio-economic development and gender equality.

Women of Dignity International (WODI) is an esteemed organization based in Canada, making a significant impact globally. Committed to promoting dignity, empowerment, and equality for women, WODI transcends borders to uplift women in communities worldwide. With a focus on women's human rights and inclusive development, WODI works tirelessly to create a world where every woman can live a life of dignity and fulfillment. Through their dedicated efforts, WODI provides support, resources, and advocacy to empower women and foster positive change. With a steadfast dedication and a global perspective, WODI stands as a catalyst for transformation, advocating for the rights and well-being of women, regardless of their background or circumstances.
